Prove the effort behind your writing

Typestamp allows you to produce proofs of writing effort. We call these 'typestamps'.

To produce a typestamp, you have to start a writing session where all your keystrokes are tracked with precise timestamps.

Once saved, your typestamp is encrypted, stored, and ready to share with a link.

When you click on 'Start session' the tracking will begin. You can pause it and resume it if needed.

The content must have no less than 200 characters.
